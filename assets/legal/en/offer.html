Public Offer Agreement

This document is an official public offer by Individual Entrepreneur Eugene Bondarev (hereinafter referred to as the "Contractor") to provide software development services.

1. Subject of the Agreement

1.1. The Contractor agrees to provide services for software development, configuration, or support (hereinafter — the "Services"), and the Customer agrees to accept and pay for these Services.

2. Service Delivery Procedure

2.1. Services are provided based on the Technical Specification (TS) agreed upon by the parties via electronic communication channels (Telegram/Email).

3. Cost and Payment

3.1. The cost of services is determined individually for each project based on complexity and scope.

4. Intellectual Property

4.1. All rights to the software code developed under this agreement are transferred to the Customer upon full payment, unless otherwise specified in the TS.
